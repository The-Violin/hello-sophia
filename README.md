## Featured: Pick Two — Proverbs Edition

Sophia picks a random chapter (10–30) and verse (1–30) from the Book of Proverbs. The player types the verse from memory and self-scores with honesty. After 5 rounds, results are saved with a timestamp.

> *"The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom."* — Proverbs 9:10

**Features:**
- Random verse selection
- Self-scoring with integrity
- Persistent score tracking
- Encouragement based on accuracy
- Timestamped game logs
